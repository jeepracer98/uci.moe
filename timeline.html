Timeline

Overview

Cal Animage Epsilon (CAE) was founded in October 1990 as a member of the Cal Animage Network and the first anime club at UC Irvine. It would evolve to become the anime club that we are today.

This timeline page is made to display the long history of the club. We derive our information and materials from limited records and first hand accounts; compiling this timeline is an ongoing process. If you are an alumni or old affiliate who might be interested in sharing a story, we encourage you to reach out!

2019 - present

As Cal Animage Epsilon’s sister-club, CAE Masquerade was renamed into the “Anime Community Club at UCI” in 2019 to reflect a broader focus on community and interactions among members. In 2020, both CAE and the Anime Community Club were forced to transfer online because of the COVID-19 pandemic.

In 2021, both clubs were re-established as simply the Anime Club @ UCI (Cal Animage Epsilon).
