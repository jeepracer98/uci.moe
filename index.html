About

Welcome to the Anime Club of UC Irvine!

We are a student-run club orientated around an interest in Anime and its related mediums. We believe that there’s more to anime when we interact, assemble, and intermingle with fellow friends and Anteaters. Thus, we hope to foster a community of Anime Anteaters at UC Irvine and abroad.

We have members with similar interests in manga, manhwa, visual novels, games, and convention-going. We are also trying to build a larger community of artists, cosplayers, and even voice actors! Whether you might be a lurking introvert or an active attendee, we welcome all friendly Anteaters and guests with open arms!

We are also known as Cal Animage Epsilon (CAE), which remains as a formal and synonymous club name. Under this title, we are proud to be UCI’s anime club and a member of the Cal Animage Network since 1990.

All club affairs – including meetings, membership, and activities – are communicated mainly through our Discord Server and our mailing list. Please join us if you’re interested!

Meetings + Screening Squad

We try to offer two different meetings per week. All friendly members, alumni, and guests are welcome to each! Our General Meetings serve as the main weekly meetings for the club where we offer optional activities, workshops, short screenings, and more.

Our club also has the Anime Club Screening Squad (or CAE Screening Squad). The Screening Squad is a subdivision of the Club that focuses primarily on screening and watching anime with others. Screening Squad meetings are typically more casual with an emphasis on consistent weekly screenings; join the Screening Squad for a more simple, relaxed, and hands-off environment! Join the Screening Squad Discord here.

Time and location for our meetings may vary per week. Please closely follow our Discord server for the latest updates (#meetings-info).

Affiliates

ZotCon (Website)
ZotCon is the anime-themed convention club at UCI. They’re responsible for holding annual anime-themed conventions at our campus. We strongly support their efforts!

CalAnime Newsletter (Website)
The CalAnime Newsletter is a newsletter founded by a group of high schoolers with a passion to keep Californian anime enthusiasts up to date on anime-related content. We are proud to sponsor CalAnime Newsletter and any other passion projects alike!
